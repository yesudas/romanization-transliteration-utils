aathiyilae thaevan vaanatthaiyum buumiyaiyum sirushditthaar.
buumiyaanathu ozhunginmaiyum veRumaiyumaai irundhathu; aazhatthinmael iruL irundhathu; thaeva aaviyaanavar jalatthinmael asaivaadikkondirundhaar.
thaevan veLicham undaakakkadavathu enRaar. veLicham undaayitRu.
veLicham nallathu enRu thaevan kandaar; veLichatthaiyum iruLaiyum thaevan vevvaeRaakap piritthaar.
thaevan veLichatthukkup pagal enRu paerittaar, iruLukku iravu enRu paerittaar; saayangaalamum vidiyaRkaalamumaagi muthalaam naL aayitRu.
pinbu thaevan; jalatthin matthiyil aagaayavirivu undaakakkadavathu enRum, athu jalatthininRu jalatthaip pirikkakkadavathu enRum sonnaar.
thaevan aagaayavirivai undupanni, aagaayavirivukkuk kiizhae irukkiRa jalatthiRkum aagaayavirivukku maelae irukkiRa jalatthiRkum pirivundaakkinaar; athu appadiyae aayitRu.
thaevan aagaayavirivukku vaanam enRu paerittaar; saayangaalamum vidiyaRkaalamumaagi, irandaam naL aayitRu.
pinbu thaevan: vaanatthin kiizhae irukkiRa jalam ooridatthil saeravum, vettaandharai kaaNappadavum kadavathu enRaar; athu appadiyae aayitRu.
